Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

IntroHater ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Stremio addon and website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Real-Debrid API Key: If you choose to use Real-Debrid integration, your API key is stored locally in your Stremio configuration and is only used to authenticate requests on your behalf.
  • Skip Segment Contributions: When you submit skip segments, we store the video identifier (IMDb ID), timing data, and an anonymized user identifier.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Statistics: Aggregated, anonymous statistics about skip segment usage to improve accuracy.
  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs including IP addresses, which are retained for security purposes and automatically deleted after 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the IntroHater service
  • Generate modified HLS manifests for skip functionality
  • Improve skip segment accuracy through community contributions
  • Display leaderboards and contribution statistics
  • Protect against abuse and ensure service security

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

IntroHater integrates with:

  • Real-Debrid: Your API key is sent directly to Real-Debrid's servers for authentication. We do not store or log your API key on our servers.
  • OMDb API: Used to fetch show/movie metadata. No personal data is shared.

6. Content Responsibility

IntroHater is a metadata service that provides timing information for skip segments. We do not host, stream, download, or distribute any video content. Users are solely responsible for the content they access through their own streaming services and debrid accounts.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection by not using the service

To exercise these rights, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

8. Children's Privacy

IntroHater is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
